Email Validation Rules

Required Field → Email should not be empty.

Valid Format → Must match standard email pattern (user@domain.com).

At least one "@" symbol.

At least one "." after "@".

Domain name length must be valid.

No Spaces Allowed → Leading, trailing, or inside spaces are invalid.

Length Limit → Typically up to 254 characters max (per RFC standard).

Valid Characters → Should only allow standard email characters: letters, digits, _, ., -, +.

✅ Password Validation Rules

(These can vary depending on project requirements, but here’s the standard secure set.)

Required Field → Password should not be empty.

Minimum Length → Usually 8 characters (sometimes 6 for less strict apps).

Maximum Length → Often 64–128 characters (to avoid DoS or storage issues).

Uppercase Letter Rule → At least 1 uppercase (A–Z).

Lowercase Letter Rule → At least 1 lowercase (a–z).

Number Rule → At least 1 digit (0–9).

Special Character Rule → At least 1 special character (!@#$%^&*()_+=-[]{};:'",.<>/?\|).

No Spaces (Optional) → Many systems disallow whitespace in passwords.

No Common/Weak Passwords → Prevent "123456", "password", "qwerty", etc.

Confirm Password Match → If you have a "Confirm Password" field, both must match.
